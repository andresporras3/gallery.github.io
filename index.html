About the Photographer

John Royal was born in 1983 in Phoenix, Arizona. At a very young age, he found his passion for photography when he won a Nikon 35Ti film camera at a local bingo. He was always a fan of using contrasts with shadows and lightings to get the best exposure of the element being photographed. Even though his parents didn't approve, when he graduated highschool, he decided to major in photography in the University of Arizona. Though his studies were fundamental for his success, his talent is natural.
